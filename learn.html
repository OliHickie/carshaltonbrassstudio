Carshalton Brass Studio is run by Cai and Victoria Isfryn. We both studied trumpet performance at the Royal Northern College of Music (Bmus) from 2006 to 2010. Victoria followed this with a masters at the Royal Conservatoire of Scotland and I at the Guildhall School of Music and Drama. We both have over 11 years experience of teaching all brass instruments at all levels and have led workshops for organisations such at the National Youth Orchestra (where we worked with the most talented young musicians in the country) Halle Outreach, LSO Discovery, Live Music Now (for whom we created projects for disadvantaged children), National Youth Brass Band of Wales, “Codi’r Tô” and “The Nucleo Project” (which are both El Sistema programmes aimed at children of a poorer economic background.) Teaching roles have included Head of Brass at Shrewsbury School and St. Catherine’s Girls School, Bramley as well as Brent Music Service, Sutton Music Service and Buckingham Music Trust. As performers we’ve played with such groups as the Royal Liverpool Philharmonic Orchestra, Royal Philharmonic Orchestra, Halle, Bournemouth Symphony, Black Dyke Band, had contracts on musicals such as Wicked UK tour, Miss Saigon Uk tour and commercially appeared with Rod Stewart, Bastille and Public Service Broadcasting. Cai has three published compositions for brass bands with Prima Vista Musik and a vast back catalogue of brass arrangements, most notably for the ten piece band ‘Old Dirty Brasstards’ as well as a two books of brass duets and trios.

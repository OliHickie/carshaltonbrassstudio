About the Studio

Carshalton Brass Studio (or ‘the garage’ as it was known then) was built in early 2020 as all lessons moved online. Located at the bottom of the garden, sound proofed and treated, it was initially an act of kindness to the neighbours as our teaching hours meant there was music to be heard all day every day from the house. The studio is now equipped to produce the finest online audio possible and is spacious enough for group, as well as individual, lessons.

We provide instrument hire options as well as a rehearsal and recording space. Our online ensembles, such as the #TheBigBrassPlayalong, have proven hugely popular with participants from all across the UK taking part.

Music theory and brass arranging lessons are taught as well as any brass instrument. Carshalton Brass Studio has self published a book of brass arrangements entitled 'Duets' as well as 'Trios' and have three pieces for full brass band published with Prima Vista Music.
